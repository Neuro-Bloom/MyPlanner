# Astrocade UGC Algorithm

> How Games Are Reviewed, Tested, and Promoted
> 

This document explains the algorithm on how games move through Astrocade after submission and how distribution is determined.

Our core principle is simple: **games players enjoy more get more reach.**

## 1. Submission & Manual Review

After submission, every game goes through a manual review to ensure:

- It works properly on **mobile devices and desktop**
- Core gameplay is functional
- Content follows Astrocade’s **safety and content guidelines**

Approved games move on to testing.

---

## 2. Initial Testing

Approved games are shown to a **small group of players** (and in Playtest sections) to gather early performance data.

Key early metrics include:

- Play duration
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Engagement signals

---

## 3. Scaled Distribution

Games with strong early performance are shown to **more players**.

At this stage, games will be shown to more players:

- The **TikTok-style feed**
- The **New** section

Metrics are continuously evaluated and may evolve over time.

---

## 4. Featured & Top Picks

Games that exceed a higher performance bar are promoted to **Featured** and **Top Picks**.

These games show consistently strong player engagement and receive the highest visibility on Astrocade.

---

## Creator Takeaway

- Early player engagement matters most
- Optimize for fun, clarity, and mobile-first gameplay
- The better players respond, the more your game is promoted
